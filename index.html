How fast does your soil carbon bucket fill? ↗
An interactive tool for exploring how microbial carbon use efficiency (CUE) and MAOC saturation interact to determine the volume and durability of soil carbon gains. Adjust soil parameters with sliders to see how much accumulates as durable mineral-associated carbon versus less permanent particulate fractions — with outputs framed for producers, project developers, and ratings agencies.
