High-CUE Opportunity Zone
Arid & cold sites (Rh < 340 gC m⁻² yr⁻¹) have elevated CUE (0.35–0.55). Adding organic inputs here produces proportionally more microbial biomass → SOC. Kenya, Saskatchewan drylands.
⚡
Rh Threshold Effect
Beyond ~340 gC m⁻² yr⁻¹, CUE plateaus at ≈0.27 ± 0.11 regardless of Rh gains. Most temperate and tropical project sites fall here — sequestration depends on mineral stabilisation, not CUE.
⚠
Market Implication
Productive-ecosystem projects (high Rh, saturated CUE) may over-attribute microbial pathways. Projects in arid/cold zones have stronger biological basis for SOC gains via CUE.
